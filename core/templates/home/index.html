FullCalendar Integration

Through a library called FullCalendar, this website allows you to make your own robotics schedule. Have Fun :)

Chat Integration

Through the use of MongoDB, this website has created its very own chat service so that you can chat with others about anything robotics.

Charts Integration

Through the library called ChartJS, this website is able to make beautiful charts about your team as well as other teams performance in VEX.

Charts

The Linegraph is to graph your performance over time. For example, we can graph your matches over your season. This will be very useful for scouting at events, because you now know how good the teams in your match are.

The Bar Graph is used in the Skills Part of the website. We can graph your top scores in the skills division and let you compare it with other teams.

The Bar Graph is also seen in the Rankings page, where you can see each event and the rankings of each team. This can be very useful in terms of scouting.

Similar Teams

With Matches, we calculate similar teams that have similar match averages with you. This way you can compete with them and better yourselves.

With Skills, we caluclate similar teams based on top score as well as averages. We know that averages will very a lot, therefore we made sure to have similar top score because that would be a much better way of judging a team.

With Rankings, we calculate similar teams based on teams in that event that had similar win rates.
